Generates tamper-proof QR tickets for in-store events. Tickets reserve a place in line only — not a product guarantee.

Verify tickets at the register by typing the last 4 characters — it checks instantly as you type. Fake or already-used tickets are caught immediately.

At the register
1Tap Verify at the bottom.
2Type the last 4 characters of the ticket code. It checks automatically — no button needed.
3Read the result, then the box clears itself for the next customer.
Green — Valid. Tap "Mark as used." Let them through.
Yellow — Already used. Do not honor.
Red — Fake / not found. Do not honor.
Before an event
1Go to Generate. Enter release name and count. Tap Generate.
2Go to Print. Select the batch and print. Cut and hand out.
3Tap Backup and copy the text somewhere safe.
Data & saving

Auto-saves in this browser. Close and reopen on the same device and your data is still there.

If you're worried about losing data on a refresh, tap Backup, copy the text, and keep it in your Notes app. Paste it into Restore anytime to bring everything back.
