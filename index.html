Generates tamper-proof QR tickets for in-store events. Tickets reserve a place in line only — not a product guarantee.

Scan a customer's QR code at the register for instant verification. Fake or already-used tickets are caught immediately.

At the register
1 Tap Verify at the bottom of the screen.
2 Tap Scan QR Code and point the camera at the customer's ticket.
3 Read the result and act accordingly:
Green — Valid. Tap "Mark as used." Let them through.
Yellow — Already used. Do not honor.
Red — Fake / not found. Do not honor.
Before an event
1Go to Generate. Enter the release name and ticket count. Tap Generate.
2Go to Print. Select your batch and print. Cut and hand out.
3Tap Export CSV to save a backup.
Data & saving

Auto-saves in this browser. Close and reopen the app on the same device and your data will still be there.

To move data to another device, export a CSV and import it on the other device.
